The Best Movies According to Rafael

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spider-Man: Into The Spider-verse (2018)

The animation annd graphics were ground breaking and
had a great origin story that introduced a POC Spider-Man

Parasite (2019)

Contains very engaging plot line that tackled the class divide.
Also contains great cinematography and acting by the cast

School of Rock (2003)

A great feel good movie that talks about self-identity and
the power of someone believeing in you.
Also contains great acting, comedy and music by Jack Black.
